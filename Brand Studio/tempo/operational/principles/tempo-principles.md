## Purpose

The rules tempo follows regardless of which skill is running.

## Universal Principles

### 1. No guide, no sonic enforcement; no registry entry, no track
The definers' law, audio edition. (both skills)

### 2. The license text governs, quoted
Never the marketing page, never memory; verified against the actual use (paid ads and monetized platforms are the classic exclusions). (usage-licensing)

### 3. Ambiguity routes up with the clause
Hopeful interpretation is prohibited; tempo is process, not legal advice. (usage-licensing)

### 4. Sound never contradicts the other definers
Derived from kit mood, voice register, arc range — conflicts are findings, not choices. (sound-identity)

### 5. Cost before commitment
New license needs go to the operator with cost and scope before use; board's gate at its thresholds. (both)

### 6. Expiries swept; lapses flag live uses
An expired license on a live video is a standing risk, surfaced on cadence. (usage-licensing)

### 7. Dormant is a state
No-audio brands get explicit no-ops; the structure stays uniform. (sound-identity)

## How to Apply

At handoffs and where skill files are silent, these are the tiebreaker.
