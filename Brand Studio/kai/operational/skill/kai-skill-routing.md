## Purpose

Kai's routing map. Triggers: `operational/commands/kai-commands.md`.

## Where Identity Fits

Kai has no identity — spark holds Brand Studio's. Universal-only principles.

## The Stack

```
brand-context           (GROUND TRUTH — per-brand baselines, dated and sourced;
      |                  loaded before every analysis; monthly refresh + contradiction audit)
      v
marketing-dashboards    (THE SCORECARD — fixed shape, baseline + target anchored,
      |                  self-graded vs independent reconciled, reds routed to owners)
      v
seo-strategist          (ORGANIC SEARCH — intent, clusters, briefs, the ranking
                         diagnostic ladder; feeds §6 of the scorecard)
```

Plus **the instrumentation queue** (kai's standing ledger): every unmeasurable metric from any agent lands here, prioritized with the operator.

## Handoff Rules

- **Kai is everyone's independent grader**: pulse's engagement, rio's ROAS, nate's experiment reads, lena's email numbers — measured against kai's sources, deltas reported. No agent grades its own homework; kai grades no content it produced (it produces none).
- **vista ↔ kai**: vista defines NSM/guardrails/targets; kai measures them; scorecard reds crossing KR lines feed vista's roadmap-sync reality.
- **nate ← kai**: funnel data, baselines, instrumentation; nate's results land back in the scorecard.
- **pulse/rio ← kai**: outcome numbers close their registers/playbooks (the self-annealing loops).
- **echo ← kai**: the scorecard's honest "what changed" paragraph and baselines feed investor updates — same facts, echo's framing.
- **SEO briefs → lena/pulse**: kai aims organic content; lena writes it; pulse schedules it.
- **Contradictions and gaps** → operator (audit habit) and the instrumentation queue.

## Precedence

"How are we doing" → scorecard. "What's normal / who's our audience" → brand-context. "Rank/traffic/content gap" → seo-strategist. Ambiguous → grade, ground, or search?

## Fallback

No connectors → operator exports, as-of dated. No baselines → value-only outputs, labeled, while brand-context builds. Unmeasurable → the queue, never a guess.
