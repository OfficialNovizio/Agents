## Purpose

Rio's routing map. Triggers: `operational/commands/rio-commands.md`.

## Where Identity Fits

Rio has no identity — spark holds Brand Studio's. Universal-only principles.

## The Stack

```
sales-retargeting        (STRATEGY — what to retarget, segmentation, sequencing,
      |                   channel selection; honesty gotchas built in)
ad-platform-mechanics    (PLATFORM LAYER — durable principles static; volatile specs
      |                   in dated per-platform playbooks; policy lines are law)
ad-thresholds            (GUARDRAILS — daily patrol vs config rules: KILL/SCALE/ESCALATE
                          recommendations, everything logged, kai reconciles)
```

Every campaign action touches all three: strategy says what, mechanics says how-on-this-platform, thresholds says whether it lives.

## Handoff Rules

- **board → rio**: the budget envelope comes from board's fiduciary gate; rio patrols within it; envelope-crossing changes go back through board. Ad spend is spend.
- **pulse → rio**: the hooks register's proven organic posts are the paid-creative shortlist — organic evidence before paid spend.
- **pixel → rio**: ad creative production (spark-gated like everything). **lena**: ad copy through voice + humanic; email-retargeting sequences are lena's to design.
- **kai ↔ rio**: kai's independent numbers reconcile platform-reported ROAS; reconciliations refresh the playbooks; rio never grades its own homework.
- **nate ↔ rio**: new-channel and creative *experiments* are nate's (ICE backlog, A/B discipline); a proven experiment graduates to rio as a standing campaign.
- **Policy/jurisdiction**: violating requests are refused and flagged (sentinel-adjacent), never optimized.

## Precedence

"Should we run ads / what do we retarget" → strategy. "Set up / why is delivery weird" → mechanics. "Scale/kill/how are they doing" → thresholds. Ambiguous → which of what/how/whether.

## Fallback

No connectors → operator exports on cadence, as-of dated. No configured rules → NOT CONFIGURED + a proposal session, never defaults.
