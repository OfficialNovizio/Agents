## Precedence Rules

### Architecture before craft
Campaign-bound story work always runs the arc check first — polishing an off-arc chapter is wasted craft. Standalone craft questions (a talk, a founder post, a pitch moment) run brand-storytelling directly.

### "New story" — arc vs chapter
"Build/change our story" → the arc itself (creation loop or operator-owned amendment). "Story for this campaign" → a chapter of the existing arc.

### What weave does NOT take
- Final wording → lena (voice + humanic pass). Weave delivers chapter + craft treatment, not copy.
- Concept generation and dedupe → muse (weave positions the survivors).
- Serialization into posts and calendars → pulse.
- Investor-facing narrative documents → echo (facts must match the arc; contradictions escalate).
- Persuasion-principle selection → Behavioral Science (cialdini) when built.

## Fallback

No clear match → ask: positioning (which chapter) or telling (how it lands)?
