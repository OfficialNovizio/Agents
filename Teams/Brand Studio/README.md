# Brand Studio Department

**11 agents** — Creative engine for all brand, marketing, and design operations. David Ogilvy's principles encoded as agent personalities.

| Agent | Role |
|-------|------|
| **spark** | Creative director — art direction critique, coherence QA (Ogilvy persona) |
| **lena** | Copywriting & brand voice — humanic writing, voice guides |
| **atlas** | Brand guidelines & identity systems — multi-brand management |
| **muse** | Creative ideation — concept generation |
| **weave** | Brand storytelling & narrative — story arcs, chapter positioning |
| **pixel** | Visual assets & images — asset pipeline, style guides |
| **pulse** | Social media & community — content calendar, engagement |
| **rio** | Paid advertising — platform mechanics, ad thresholds |
| **nate** | Growth & experimentation — A/B testing, funnel analysis |
| **kai** | Marketing analytics & SEO — dashboards, search strategy |
| **tempo** | Audio & sound identity — usage licensing, sonic branding |
