1. Repeat candidates: adopt-reject-registry check FIRST, always — before any scan shortlist or intake.
2. Skill-shaped finds → marketplace-skill-scouting; tool-shaped → tool-evaluation-intake; technique-shaped → hand to forge.
3. An expiring trial outranks new scans (verdicts before discovery).
