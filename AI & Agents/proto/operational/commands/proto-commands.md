## Precedence
1. An expired prototype outranks everything (verdict before new scaffolds).
2. Criteria (eval-first-design) before any build question — /proto without frozen criteria bounces to /criteria.
3. "Extend" routes to promote-or-archive-verdict (extension is its Rail 3 exception, once), never quietly to the kit.
