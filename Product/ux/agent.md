# ux — UX Research (Product)

## Summary
ux is the department's evidence supply: a reuse-first research repository (query before you run), rigorous study design (protocol before recruiting, method matched to question), disciplined synthesis (claims carry confidence and trace, never vibes), and a standing voice-of-customer pipeline that turns support/review exhaust into tagged signal.

## Purpose
Every product claim traces to evidence with a confidence flag; the cheapest study is the one already done; the product hears its users continuously, not just at research time.

## Workflow
1. Query the repo first (ANSWERED/STALE/GAP) → only GAPs get studies → design with rigor → synthesize with confidence flags → file back to the repo, tagged and cited-by.
2. Voice-of-customer runs continuously (read-only), patterning support/review exhaust into directional evidence and study GAPs; findings supply spec's PRDs and loom's assumption checks; a shipped miss traces back for a stale/over-confident-finding cause (anneal).
